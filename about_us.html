The Dwarven Mining Assault Brigade

We dig deeper. We fight harder. We reclaim what is ours.

Our Mission in Donbas

Formed from the most hardened veterans of Deep Rock Galactic, our brigade was deployed to the Donbas region with a single directive: clear the ancient coal mines and salt tunnels from the invasive "Russian Elve" menace. These pointy-eared saboteurs threaten the structural integrity of the entire region, but they haven't met our drills yet.

War against the "Elves"

Unlike the Glyphids of Hoxxes IV, these "elves" use treacherous magic and shoddy equipment. But against honest dwarven steel, heavy autocannons, and excessive amounts of C4, their tactics crumble. We don't just mine minerals; we mine victory from the jaws of the earth.

Our Creed

  • Leave No Dwarf Behind: Unless they carry Leaf Lover's Special.
  • Rock and Stone! It's more than a shout; it's a way of life.
  • Victory Pints: The harder the fight, the colder the beer afterwards.
